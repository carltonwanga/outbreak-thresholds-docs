About Application

This application makes it easy for the ministry of health to detect outbreaks faster by computiong action and alert thresholds bases on cases of malaria/meningitis reported for each subcounty The alert threshold is meant to indicate that there is a possible malaria upsurge and that there is need for confirmation through laboratory investigations. The action threshold is meant to indicate a serious upsurge of Malaria to an epidemic level.

Features

Management of historic data- Reported cases
Calculation of thresholds
Synchronization and recalculation of thresholds
Analysis of thresholds and outbreak-detection results
Tracking weekly data against action and alert threshold
Confirmation of thresholds through weather data
Outbreak confirmation surveys
